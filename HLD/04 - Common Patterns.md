## Common Patterns

These patterns are not mutually exclusive

* Pushing Realtime Updates
* Managing Long-Running Tasks
* Dealing with Contention
* Scaling Reads
* Scaling Writes
* Handling Large Blobs
* Multi-step Processes
* Proximity-Based Services

### Pattern Selection

These pattern often work together to solve a complex design. A video might use Large Blob for video uploads, Realtime Updates for progress notification
